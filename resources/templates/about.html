The project

May 2015, I'm at home wanting to play a "CrackAttack" game. Bad luck, it's not installed on the computer I was using.

We're in 2015, it's time to try a web based edition... I found plenty of them, bad ones.

It was time to create my own.

That's how the idea started, the next part was to decide how to do it. From there it was easy, my web skills weren't that sharp at the time, too much maintenance without trying new technologies on my other projects. It was time to catch up and here we are : a mix of AngularJs/ThreeJs/Cocoon and some css stuff to create a fun game.

The next step ? All the features listed on github and perhaps a 2 meter wooden arcade game to put in the office !
