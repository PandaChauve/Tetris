When Will You Lose

WWYL is a game about survival in the ruthless world of falling blocks.

The concept is quite simple, you can swap blocks horizontally, when 3 or more blocks of a same color are aligned (vertically or horizontally) they're destroyed.

The ground is going up, slowly at start but the speed is steadily increasing. After one minute, blocks are starting to fall randomly.

The instant a non falling block goes completely past the red line, you lose !

Score & points

The basic unit is the line, to create one you need 3 blocks aligned.

Each line is worth one point. Each additional block on a line is 1 point (a line of 5 blocks is 3 points).

If you create a cross or a corner (an horizontal and a vertical line combined) they count as a single line. The block used to join them is counted twice.

You'll get bonus points for creating lines with falling blocks or for doing multiple lines at once.

Since it's seems so simple, why not add a small layer of complexity with combos ? Each time you get points, you increase the combo modifier. After 2 seconds without increasing it, it's reset to 1. Your score is multiplied by the combo modifier !

You should always try to chain your moves !

Single player modes

Arcade

Classic

The classic mode is a survival game in a 6X10 grid. Blocks will fall from the top and the grid is going up at an increasing speed. The more you play, the harder it gets !

Tips : at the beginning the game is slow, it's easier to create big combinations. You should use the speed key to insure a sufficient block pool for your combos. When the game starts to be too fast for you, focus on destroying small blocks, the more you play the more you score !

Sandbox

The ground won't move, the blocks won't fall. Take your time to train your combos. You can still use the "Speed up" key to add new lines to the grid.

Campaign

Tutorial

Simple exercises based on the arcade games. It's the best way to learn.

Challenges

No falling blocks, no moving ground, just some objectives and constraints. Reach a score, destroy all the blocks, destroy only one color, keep only a color,... You'll need your brain.

Time attack

You have a limited time to score a certain amount of points. Some grids are randomly generated, other are not !

Tetris attack

You have a limited amount of swaps to destroy all the blocks. This is a clone of Tetris Attack's puzzle mode, 60 levels of increasing difficulty. All the levels can be completed with 5 sec between each swap, you don't need to try skillful moves.

Multiplayer modes

You can't play them on mobile device.

Coop

Same rules as the single player mode "Wide" but you can play with a friend.

1v1

Same rules as the single player mode "Classic" with 2 players. Each time you score 3 points, some blocks will fall in your partners grid. Show that you're the best.

2v2

You'll need some gamepads to play this one, it's a mix between Coop and 1v1.
